# Cinematography Cheatsheet — Variable Comparison Prompts
**Base Subject:** A 30-year-old Asian man in a dark suit standing in an urban environment
**Goal:** Change ONE variable per set to see the visual impact

---

## SET 1: FOCAL LENGTH (Lens Compression)
*Same scene, same pose — only the lens changes*

**A) 24mm wide angle:**
`Photorealistic, a 30-year-old Asian man in a dark suit standing in a narrow Tokyo alley at dusk, shot on 24mm lens, eye level, medium wide shot, soft overcast light, neutral color grading, deep DOF, 16:9`

**B) 50mm normal:**
`Photorealistic, a 30-year-old Asian man in a dark suit standing in a narrow Tokyo alley at dusk, shot on 50mm lens, eye level, medium wide shot, soft overcast light, neutral color grading, moderate DOF, 16:9`

**C) 85mm portrait:**
`Photorealistic, a 30-year-old Asian man in a dark suit standing in a narrow Tokyo alley at dusk, shot on 85mm lens, eye level, medium close-up, soft overcast light, neutral color grading, shallow DOF, 16:9`

**D) 135mm telephoto:**
`Photorealistic, a 30-year-old Asian man in a dark suit standing in a narrow Tokyo alley at dusk, shot on 135mm lens, eye level, close-up, soft overcast light, neutral color grading, shallow DOF, 16:9`

> **What to look for:** 24mm = wide + distorted + more background. 135mm = compressed + flat + creamy bokeh. Subject "feel" changes from environmental to intimate.

`Photorealistic, close-up face filling frame, a 30-year-old Asian man in a dark suit in a rain-soaked Tokyo intersection at night, 85mm lens, eye level, shallow DOF, neon bokeh background, teal and orange grading, 2.39:1`

*Same wide shot — only the lens rendering changes*

**A) Anamorphic:**
`Photorealistic, medium shot of a 30-year-old Asian man in a suit walking through city street at night, anamorphic lens, oval bokeh, horizontal lens flare from street lights, 2.39:1, shallow DOF, teal grading`

**B) Spherical:**
`Photorealistic, medium shot of a 30-year-old Asian man in a suit walking through city street at night, spherical lens, circular bokeh, no flare, 16:9, shallow DOF, teal grading`

**C) Vintage / uncoated:**
`Photorealistic, medium shot of a 30-year-old Asian man in a suit walking through city street at night, vintage uncoated lens, warm flares, lower contrast, soft organic rendering, cat-eye bokeh, 16:9, shallow DOF`

**D) Fisheye:**
`Photorealistic, medium shot of a 30-year-old Asian man in a suit walking through city street at night, fisheye lens, 180 degree barrel distortion, deep DOF, dramatic perspective, 16:9`

> **What to look for:** Anamorphic = oval blur + horizontal flares (Hollywood). Spherical = round clean bokeh. Vintage = dreamy/soft/warm. Fisheye = extreme distortion.

## MASTER COMPARISON: Same Scene, Different Directors

**Kubrick style:**
`Photorealistic, Kubrick style, a 30-year-old Asian man in a suit walking down a long hotel corridor, single-point perspective, centered framing, 24mm wide lens, symmetrical composition, cold neutral grading, deep DOF, eye level, locked off tripod, slow creep push in, 4:3 aspect ratio`

**Fincher style:**
`Photorealistic, Fincher style, a 30-year-old Asian man in a suit walking down a long hotel corridor, 40mm lens, slightly low angle, teal cyan desaturated grading, crushed blacks, low-key lighting, precise dolly tracking, shallow DOF, 2.39:1 widescreen`

**Wong Kar-wai style:**
`Photorealistic, Wong Kar-wai style, a 30-year-old Asian man in a suit walking down a long hotel corridor, 24mm wide lens, handheld, saturated warm color, step-printed motion blur, neon color accents, shallow DOF, 1.85:1`

**Wes Anderson style:**
`Photorealistic, Wes Anderson style, a 30-year-old Asian man in a suit standing in a hotel corridor, centered framing, 40mm lens, planimetric flat staging, pastel color grading, symmetrical composition, soft diffused light, deep DOF, 4:3 aspect ratio`

**Christopher Nolan style:**
`Photorealistic, Christopher Nolan style, a 30-year-old Asian man in a suit walking down a long hotel corridor, IMAX 65mm, 35mm wide lens, deep DOF, practical lighting, natural exposure, epic scale, grounded realism, 1.43:1 IMAX ratio`

> **What to look for:** Same corridor + same person = 5 completely different movies. This is the power of cinematography language in prompts.
